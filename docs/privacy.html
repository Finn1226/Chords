Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025‑09‑13

Overview

Chords does not collect, store, or sell personal data. The app displays chord diagrams bundled with the app and performs basic operations entirely on-device.

Data Collection

We do not collect analytics or tracking data, and we do not include advertising SDKs. The app does not require an account or login.

Network Access

The app may access the network for standard platform services (e.g., fetching updates distributed via the App Store or EAS Update). This traffic uses standard, exempt encryption (HTTPS/TLS).

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at chordfanta@gmail.com.
